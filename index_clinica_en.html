About us

We create Medcare Clinic Private Ambulance to help patients that cannot use the Public Ambulance Service. Even though it might not be a medical emergency, some patients still need ambulance transportation.

Quality service

At Medcare Clinic our main values are profesionalism, punctuality, empathy and respect for our patients, offering great attention to safety and hygiene.

 

The ambulances we use are modern and are equipped with stretcher, wheelchair, portable oxygen cylinders, etc. We have all the accessories needed for a safe and comfy trip.

Our potential clients might need:

  • transportation after medical discharge or before hospitalisation
  • transportation to and from clinics for medical investigations or consult, dialysis, etc.
  • treatment in recovery clinics for locomotor disorders
  • to be present at special events (weddings, funerals, christening) and have disabilities.
  • to be present at sports events
